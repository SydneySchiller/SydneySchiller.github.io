Re:Search

By far my biggest, and most time consuming project, Re:Search is a game created for the UC Davis Biomedical Department, with the main goal of preparing university freshmen for undergraduate research. Through a series of chapters within the game, the player is introduced to historical research figures and common research methods and practices.

My role is this project is to create the environment art and the art for the user interface. To create the environment art I first designed the level, then created the tiled within Aseprite, compiled the tiles and set the colliders within Tiled, and then imported the completed maps to Unity.
