Who I am

Hello! My name is Sydney Schiller. I am a recent graduate of the University of California, Davis with a BS in computer science. While attending university, I pursued my passion for video game and app development through coursework and personal projects. This passion led me to gain experience using software and tools that relate to a computer graphics environment.

While computer science is a large part of who I am, I also enjoy creating art and seeing it come to life in a video game or 2D animation. While pursuing my degree, I supplemented my learning by taking classes in traditional studio art, pixel and digital art, as well as 3D model creation and animation.

Using my skills in both computer science and art, I have worked as the middle-man between the programming and art teams for many projects, helping to bridge the gap to bring the project to the desired polish. I would love to continue using these two skills in tandem to create great products.
