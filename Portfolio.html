Re:Search, A Campus Story

Re:Search is a game created for the UC Davis Biomedical Department, with the main goal of preparing university freshmen for undergraduate research. Through a series of chapters within the game, the player is introduced to historical research figures and common research methods and practices.

My role in this project was to create the environment art for all 8 levels. This involved designing the layout of each level, creating all the assets within Aseprite, setting the colliders, and importing the maps into Unity.
